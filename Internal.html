Internal Migration:

Internal migration refers to the movement of people from one place to another within the same country. People may choose to internally migrate for various reasons such as seeking better job opportunities, education, better living conditions, or to be closer to family members. For example, someone moving from a rural area to a city within their own country is considered internal migration.
