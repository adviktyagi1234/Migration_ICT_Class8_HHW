Forced Migration:

Forced migration happens when people are compelled to leave their homes and move to a new place because of dangers like war, persecution, or natural disasters. Unlike choosing to move for better opportunities, forced migration occurs because staying in their current location is no longer safe or possible. People who experience forced migration often seek safety and protection in another country or region where they can live without fear or danger.
